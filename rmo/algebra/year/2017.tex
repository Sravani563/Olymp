P(x) = x^2 + \frac{1}{2}x + b
\end{align*} 
and
\begin{align*} 
Q(x) = x^2 + cx + d 
\end{align*}
be two polynomials with real coefficients such that P(x)Q(x) = Q(P(x)) for all real x. Find all the real roots of P(Q(x)) = 0.
